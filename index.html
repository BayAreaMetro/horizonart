Art on the Horizon Contest

The Metropolitan Transportation Commission and the Association of Bay Area Governments are now accepting submissions to Art on the Horizon: Youth Confronting Climate Change. The theme of the contest is, What can you do to stop climate change? We are looking for photography and video submissions from people aged 13 to 23 that demonstrate what you, your family, friends and community can do to combat climate change.

All entries must be received by 5 p.m. PST on March 22, 2019. See Official Rules for details.
